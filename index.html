About me

Currently, I am a post-doc at Dr. Sachiko Kiyama's lab at Tohoku University, investigating the neural basis for language processing and visuospatial development within Chinese-script bilinguals. I received my PhD degree and Master degree in Japanese Language and Culture under the supervision of Prof. Katsuo Tamaoka at Nagoya University. Before that, I earned my Bachelor degree in Japanese (Language and Culture) at Dalian University of Foreign Languages.
